Welcome to the MRC and Free Speech America!

The Media Research Center has led the fight against media bias for 30 years, but three years ago we also launched FREE SPEECH AMERICA to lead the charge against censorship of conservatives by Facebook, Twitter, Amazon, Google, YouTube, and others.

Big Tech has waged war against conservatives, going as far as suspending and banning former President Donald Trump from many platforms. If this can happen to the President, it can happen to any one of us. We must protect free speech and let them know we will not be silenced!
